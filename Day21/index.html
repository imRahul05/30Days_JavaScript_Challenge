About Me

I'm Rahul Kumar, a passionate frontend developer with expertise in React.js and Flutter. I have developed several interactive and responsive web applications, including my personal portfolio and a blog platform with features like user authentication, commenting, and upvoting. I am driven by a commitment to user-centric design, innovation, and creating impactful, scalable digital solutions.
